About the Project

The CCL helps students, researchers, and creators transparently document the role of artificial intelligence across all stages of their creative process. Inspired by the spirit of Creative Commons, the CCL promotes honest reflection, responsible use of AI, and clarity in collaborative, tech-assisted creation.

Levels of AI Contribution

0
Full Human Work

This phase was completed entirely by the human without AI assistance.

1
AI for Insight

AI was used for research, inspiration, or brainstorming. The human crafted the final work.

2
AI for Drafting

AI generated early outputs that were then edited or rebuilt by the human.

3
AI as Co-Creator

An iterative collaboration between human and AI shaped the outcome together.

4
AI as Driver

AI generated the majority of the content; the human curated and edited.
