0 - Full Human Work

1 - AI for Insight

2 - AI for Drafting

3 - AI as Co-Creator

A collaborative process where the student and AI iterated together, refining outputs through mutual feedback.

4 - AI as Driver

AI produced most of the work in this phase, while the student acted primarily as a curator, editor, or selector.
